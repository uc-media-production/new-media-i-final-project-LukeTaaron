About Us Page

Together, Max, Samantha, and Dexter form a dynamic team that not only understands the gaming culture but also excels in their respective roles to make the arcade bar a thriving and innovative establishment.

Our Team

Max "Pixel" Rodriguez

Co-Founder and Creative Director

Max, known as "Pixel" in the gaming community, is a passionate gamer and graphic designer. With a background in game development, Max brings a keen eye for design and aesthetics to the arcade bar. As the Creative Director, he is responsible for curating the gaming experience, ensuring that the arcade games, decor, and overall ambiance align with the latest gaming trends. Max is also the brains behind organizing gaming tournaments and themed events, making the arcade bar a go-to spot for gaming enthusiasts.

pixel@arcadelegacy.com

Samantha "Joystick" Morgan

Co-Founder and Operations Manager

Samantha, nicknamed "Joystick," is a seasoned business professional with a love for gaming culture. Having managed successful bars and restaurants in the past, she brings a wealth of experience in operations and customer service. Samantha oversees the day-to-day operations of the arcade bar, from staffing to inventory management. She ensures that the bar runs smoothly and that customers have a seamless and enjoyable experience. Samantha is also in charge of creating partnerships with local breweries and game developers to bring unique offerings to the arcade bar.

joystick@arcadelegacy.com

Dexter "High Score" Rollins

Co-Founder and Technical Director

Dexter, known as "High Score" among friends, is a tech guru and avid gamer. With a background in IT and software development, Dexter takes charge of the technical aspects of the arcade bar. He's responsible for maintaining and upgrading the arcade machines, ensuring they are in top-notch condition. Dexter also implements cutting-edge technologies, such as virtual reality gaming stations and interactive displays, to keep the arcade bar ahead of the curve. His passion for gaming technology makes the arcade bar a hub for gamers seeking the latest and greatest in the gaming world.

highscore@arcadelegacy.com
